Rajgir

Rajgir, meaning "The City of Kings," is a historic town in the district of Nalanda in Bihar, India. As the ancient seat and capital of the Haryanka dynasty, the Pradyota dynasty, the Brihadratha dynasty and the Mauryan Empire, as well as the dwelling ground of such historical figures as The Buddha and The Mahavira, the city holds a place of prominence in Hindu, Buddhist and Jain scriptures.

Kaua Kol

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Kauakol village is 257777. Kauakol village is located in Kawakol subdivision of Nawada district in Bihar, India. It is situated 46km away from district headquarter Kawakol. Kawakol is the sub-district headquarter of Kauakol village. As per 2009 stats, Kauakole is the gram panchayat of Kauakol village.
